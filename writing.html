The Terror at Brickhaven

"...The symbol was a star, though it had more parts than just a star. It contained in it several triangles of different shapes and each could have possessed their own soul but together, it was an entanglement of life and death, a metaphor perhaps understood in those ways which even words could not express. In the middle of the star stood a single open eye, whose strange markings which encircled it seemed to dance before me. The eye on its own caused a dread feeling deep inside me, bearing some form of life to the point that I became afraid to look away in case it might blink."

"...Aside from the relentless patter of rain on everything within earshot, there were no sounds emanating from Brickhaven. The town seemed a desolate heath in absence of all activity. The houses, however, were most definitely inhabited, and with the lack of human presence anywhere in sight, it was most certain that Brickhaven's inhabitants must have occupied at least one of the buildings I could see. The shutters had all been drawn and the town remained to me a void of life."
