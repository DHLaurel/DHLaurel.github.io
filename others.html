Music Providers

Our generation is seemingly one that puts music at its core. It's important to me to know the voice my fellows subscribe to - and it seems very disconcerting therefore that so many of them seem to cling to the voices pushed on them by large corporations and monetary awards ceremonies. Nevertheless, here are some of the individuals whose tastes in music gave me hope:
